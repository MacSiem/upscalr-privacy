Upscalr — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

Polska wersja

1. Overview

App operator: Maciej Siemiński. Public privacy contact: mail@macsiem.dev.

Upscalr is a mobile app for improving image quality, clarity, and resolution on iOS and Android. The app is designed around local processing: selected photos and images stay on your device during import, enhancement, saving, and sharing.

2. Photos and Image Content

Upscalr reads images that you explicitly select from your gallery or file picker. The image content is processed locally on the device. We do not operate a server that receives, stores, or processes your photos.

  • Photos are not uploaded by Upscalr for enhancement.
  • Results are saved to your gallery only when you choose to save them.
  • Sharing uses the system share sheet and is controlled by the destination you choose.

3. Local Storage

The app may store local settings such as onboarding completion and device processing benchmark data. This data remains on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app or clearing app data where supported by the platform.

4. Permissions

Upscalr requests photo library access so you can choose images and save improved results. Permission prompts are handled by iOS or Android. You can change these permissions in system settings at any time.

5. Crash Diagnostics

Upscalr can use Sentry for crash reporting when a build is configured with a Sentry DSN. Crash diagnostics may include app version, OS version, device model, stack traces, and navigation breadcrumbs. Screenshots, view hierarchy capture, and image content attachment are disabled in the app configuration.

6. Advertising

The current app code reserves an in-app processing ad area, but Google AdMob is not integrated in Upscalr at the time of this policy update. If third-party advertising is enabled in a future build, this policy and the App Store / Google Play privacy declarations must be updated before release.

7. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or account credentials in the app.
  • We do not upload photo or image content for processing.
  • We do not collect precise location data.
  • We do not sell personal information.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact mail@macsiem.dev.
